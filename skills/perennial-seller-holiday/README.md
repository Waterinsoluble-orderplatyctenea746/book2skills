# Perennial Seller — Holiday's Longevity Framework

A Claude skill that encodes Ryan Holiday's framework from *Perennial Seller* for creating work that earns continued success over years and decades. It covers all four phases: creative process (making work worth making), positioning (packaging for longevity), marketing (building word-of-mouth engines), and platform (building owned audiences that compound).

## What It Does

- Diagnoses whether a creative project has the foundations for long-term success or is optimized for short-term attention
- Runs the one-liner test and longevity assessment for any project
- Develops positioning strategy: the one sentence, paragraph, and page descriptions that make work findable and shareable
- Builds a marketing and platform strategy oriented toward years-long growth rather than a single launch spike

## When to Use It

- "Will this [book/product/course/creative work] have long-term staying power?"
- "How do I position my work for lasting success rather than a quick launch?"
- "Help me write the one-liner / elevator pitch for my project"
- "How do I market something without a big budget, over time?"
- "Should I chase this trend or focus on depth and timelessness?"
- "How do I build a platform and find my first 1,000 true fans?"
- "How do I test my idea before fully committing to it?"
