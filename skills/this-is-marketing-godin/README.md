# This Is Marketing — Godin's Framework

A Claude skill that encodes Seth Godin's marketing philosophy from *This Is Marketing*. It rejects mass-market interruption in favor of generous, specific, and empathetic work: finding the smallest viable audience, deeply understanding their worldview, earning permission, and creating change that spreads organically.

## What It Does

- Helps you define your smallest viable audience through psychographic profiling, not demographics
- Diagnoses why marketing isn't resonating by identifying worldview mismatches and status/affiliation dynamics
- Builds your marketing promise statement: who it's for, what they believe, what transformation you offer
- Maps your audience's position on the permission ladder and identifies the next step toward evangelism

## When to Use It

- "Who is my target audience / how do I niche down?"
- "Why isn't my content connecting with people?"
- "How do I build trust and grow an email list?"
- "Should I go broad or go specific with my marketing?"
- "Help me find the right positioning for my product"
- "How do I create a loyal community / tribe?"
- "What's my marketing promise?"
- "How do I price this in a way that signals value?"

## Installation

1. Download or clone this folder.
2. In Claude.ai, go to Settings → Skills and upload the skill folder.
3. The skill will appear in your available skills list.
